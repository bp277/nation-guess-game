Guess the mystery nation!

  • You have eight attempts to guess an official UN member state.
  • If the country you guessed has a green match in the continent, subregion, or bordering countries column, you have guessed correctly.
  • If the country you guessed has a yellow match in the population, size, or bordering country column, the mystery country has a smaller population, less land area, or fewer neighboring countries than the country you guessed.
  • If the country you guessed has a red match in the population, size, or bordering country column, the mystery country has a larger population, more land area, or more neighboring countries than the country you guessed.
  • If you are stuck, you may find it helpful to look at the coat of arms or the flag of the country for a hint.
  • Good luck!
